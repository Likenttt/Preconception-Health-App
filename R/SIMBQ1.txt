<p><strong>Since guys can</strong><strong>’t get pregnant, what are some of the things they can do for better preconception health?</strong></p>
<p>Here are some things men can do for their own health and for the women and children in their lives from the Center for Disease Control. Make a plan and take action. Prevent and treat sexually transmitted infections. Stop smoking, using street drugs, and drinking excessive amounts of alcohol. Be careful about toxic or harmful substances. Prevent infertility.&nbsp; Reach and maintain a healthy weight. Learn your family history. Get help for violence. Get mentally healthy. Support your partner. Click the link below for details on each of them.</p>
